Admission Information

Welcome to Lydols Academy, located in Cape Coast, Ghana! We're excited to welcome your child to our school community. Below is a list of items we require parents to bring when admitting their child:

  • Original birth certificate of the child
  • Immunization records of the child
  • Two recent passport-size photographs of the child
  • Parent's or guardian's photo ID (e.g., driver's license, national ID)
  • Completed admission forms (if provided by the school)
  • Proof of address (utility bill or rental agreement)
  • Any relevant academic records or transfer certificates (if applicable)
  • Registration fee (if applicable)

Please ensure that you have all the necessary documents and items listed above when you come to our school to admit your child. If you have any questions or require further assistance, feel free to contact us.

We look forward to meeting you and your child soon!
